Hyper Tunnel Mod

Explanation

This mod adds the game Hyper Tunnel to Minecraft PE. To start a new game, type "/hypertunnel start" in the chat. Make sure the area around you is flat (Preferably a flat world), otherwise you won't be able to see the game because there are other blocks in the way. The game works very simple. You have to prevent the gold cube from running into the stone. You can move the gold cube up and down with your arrow keys. The stone will move faster as you get further into the game. If you run into stone, the game will end and say your score. See if you can beat my 633 :) If you don't fully understand it, please watch the video below.
