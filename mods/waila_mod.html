WAILA Mod

Explanation

This mod adds in a small text under your health bar, which tells you what block you're looking at and what ID and damage value it has (With split controls enabled, it's the block in the middle of the cross, with split controls disabled it's the block you touch with your finger when you mine/drag the screen around). It also works for blocks that are added in by mods.
