More Pistons Mod

Explanation

This mod adds 9 pistons into Minecraft PE 0.13 which work with vanilla redstone. It adds normal and sticky pistons that extend 1-4 blocks and a ultra sticky piston, which pulls all the attached blocks instead of just one when it collapses

Crafting Recipes

Piston:
3 Wooden Planks, 4 Cobblestone, 1 Redstone, 1 Iron
Sticky Piston:
1 Piston, 1 Slime Ball
Double, Triple, Quadruple Pistons:
2-4 Pistons
Double, Triple, Quadruple Sticky Pistons:
2-4 Sticky Pistons
Ultra Sticky Piston: 1 Piston, 8 Slime Balls
