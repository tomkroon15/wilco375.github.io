Elevator Mod

Explanation

This mod adds an elevator block to Minecraft PE. To get started: Place 2 or more elevator blocks above each other with a maximum of 16 blocks between them. Jump to go to the first elevator block above the one you're standing on. To go down to the first available elevator block under the one you're standing on, press the 'down' button in the bottom-right corner, that appears whenever you're standing on an elevator block.
