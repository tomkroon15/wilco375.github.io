Walker Rings Mod

Explanation

This mod adds in 3 rings. If you are holding such ring in your hand you can walk over water, lava or air, similar to the Frost Walker enchantment on Minecraft PC.
As soon as you come near water with the Ring of the Frost Walker all the water blocks one block below you will freeze temporarily:


The same goes for the Ring of the Fire Walker, but this time it allows you to walk over lava:


Last but certainly not least, the Ring of the Air Walker allows you to walk in the air, by temporarily turning all air blocks under your feet into glass:

The Ring of the Frost Walker and the Ring of the Fire Walker can be crafted using 8 iron ingots and 1 diamond.
The Ring of the Air Walker can be crafted with 6 iron ingots and 3 diamonds.
