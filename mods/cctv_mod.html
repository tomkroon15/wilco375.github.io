CCTV Security Camera Mod

Explanation

You can craft a camera with a piece of glass, 2 redstone and 6 stone, and a Monitor with 7 stone, a piece of glass and a piece of redstone. If you place it down it propts you to give it a name. After that, you can type '/cctv show <camera>', or click a block with the Monitor and select the camera from the GUI. You can find all the commands with '/cctv list'. You can watch the video down here for more explanation.
