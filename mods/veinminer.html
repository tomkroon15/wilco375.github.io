VeinMiner Mod

Explanation

This mod allows you to mine an entire vein of blocks at once.
You can enable it by holding a pickaxe, axe or shovel and clicking the V button that pops up in the bottom-right corner (0.11 and earlier) or by sneaking (0.12 and later)
If you mine one of a certain type of block, all the connected blocks of the same type will also break (up to 128 blocks at a time).
Also V1.2 fully works with 0.13 enchantments and custom names.
