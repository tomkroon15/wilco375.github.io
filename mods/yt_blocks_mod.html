Youtuber Blocks Mod

Explanation

You can now craft your favourite Youtubers in Minecraft!
Step or click on the blocks to get effects.
To get started find some Youtuber Ore, which randomly spawns in your world, and smelt it.
You'll get a Youtuber Essence which you have to surround with one of the following items in a crafting table:
