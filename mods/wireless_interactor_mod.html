Wireless Interactor Mod

Explanation

You can craft a Wireless Interactor with a chest and 8 redstone, and a Wireless Connector with 7 stone, a piece of glass and a piece of redstone. If you place an interactor down it prompts you to give it a name. After that, you can click a block with the connector and select the interactor from the GUI. You can find all the commands with '/wi list'. You can watch the video down here for more explanation.
