DecoLights Mod

Explanation

This mod adds 4 new lights into Minecraft PE:
Lantern:

The lantern can be placed on all 6 sides of the block, and will render differently depending on the side of the block it's placed on.

The Ceiling Light:

This light can only be placed on the ceiling.

Old Street Light:

This street light can be placed on the top of a block.

Modern Street Light:

This street light has to be placed by clicking on the side of a block. The light will face away from the block you clicked.
