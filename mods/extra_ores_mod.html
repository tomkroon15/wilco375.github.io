Extra Ores Mod

Explanation

This mod adds in 3 ores: Gunpowder Ore, which will drop one Gunpowder (But also has a 5% chance of exploding!), Rainbow Ore, which will drop 4 dyes of a random color (excluding Lapis Lazulli), and Bone Ore, which will drop two bones
